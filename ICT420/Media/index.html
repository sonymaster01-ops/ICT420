About Me

I am from a small town in northeast Ohio called Upper Sandusky. I lived there for about eighteen years until I moved to Lexington. I have two bulldogs back home named Diesel and Tank. I currently travel the country doing low voltage construction work, all while being a student at UK and a member of the Kentucky National Guard.
